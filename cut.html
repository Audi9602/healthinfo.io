Abrasion

It occurs when your skin rubs or scrapes against a rough or hard surface.

Puncture

A puncture is a small hole caused by a long, pointy object, such as a nail or needle.

Abrasion
Your skin gets rubbed or torn off, for example scraped knee of a kid after falling on concrete floor.
Puncture
Your affected skin part gets a bit swollen and red usually, for example piercing finger by needle.
